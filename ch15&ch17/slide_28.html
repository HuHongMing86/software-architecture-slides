Conclusion: Microservices offer unparalleled agility and scalability, but introduce significant distributed system complexity and performance overhead.

Microservices – Key Trade-offs to Consider

Strengths
  • Extreme Deployability: Independent deployment of small services enables rapid, reliable releases.
  • High Scalability & Elasticity: Scale only the specific services that experience high load.
  • Evolutionary Design: Easy to replace, rewrite, or retire individual services without affecting the whole system.
Weaknesses
  • Distributed Complexity: Network reliability, latency, and security become major concerns.
  • Performance Overhead: Network hops and data serialization impact overall system speed.
  • Data Consistency: Managing distributed transactions requires complex patterns like Sagas.
