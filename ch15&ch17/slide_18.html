Bounded Context Drives Service Granularity Decisions

Finding the right service boundaries

Finding the right service granularity is one of the biggest challenges in microservices. Architects often make services too small, requiring excessive inter-service communication.
Key Advice
Iteration is the only way to ensure good service design. Architects rarely find the perfect granularity on the first attempt.
Purpose
Each microservice should have very high functional cohesion, representing one significant behavior of the application.
Transactions
Entities that need to collaborate within a transaction often indicate good service boundaries. Avoid distributed transactions.
Choreography
If a set of services requires extensive communication to function, consider combining them into a larger service.
