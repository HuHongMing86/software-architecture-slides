Key Takeaways

Space-Based Architecture
Achieves near-infinite scalability and high performance by removing the central database constraint and relying on replicated in-memory data grids with asynchronous data pumps.
Microservices Architecture
Achieves extreme deployability and domain decoupling by modeling bounded contexts as physically independent, single-purpose services, each owning its own data.
