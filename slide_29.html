Comparing Space-Based and Microservices Architecture

Architectural Choice
Choose Space-Based Architecture when extreme performance and elasticity under unpredictable load are the primary drivers. Choose Microservices when domain decoupling, independent deployability, and evolutionary design are paramount.
Criteria Space-Based Architecture Microservices Architecture
Primary Goal High performance & scalability High decoupling & domain modeling
Data Management Replicated in-memory grids Strict data isolation per service
Scalability Near-infinite (no DB bottleneck) High (independent scaling)
Communication Asynchronous data pumps Protocol-aware (REST, gRPC, events)
Key Challenge Data collisions & cache consistency Distributed transactions & workflows
