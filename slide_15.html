Space-Based Architecture – Key Trade-offs

Strengths
  • Near-infinite scalability through in-memory data caching and elimination of database constraints.
  • High concurrency: Handles millions of concurrent users effortlessly.
  • Deployment flexibility: Supports hybrid cloud and on-premise deployments.
Weaknesses
  • High complexity due to eventual consistency between caches and the primary data store.
  • Testing challenges: Testing at scale is extremely complex and expensive; most high-volume testing occurs in production.
  • High cost due to caching product licensing fees and high resource utilization.
  • Data loss risk if any component crashes without proper safeguards.
